Vincennes Wood resume

The Vincennes Wood is one of the two "green lungs" of the capital. It is located in the east of Paris, on the edge of the 12th arrondissement. Numerous organizations installed in the Bois de Vincennes allow you to learn more and get closer to the biodiversity of the site:
the École du Breuil and its arboretum,the Jardin d'agronomie tropicale, the Ferme de Paris, the Maison Paris Nature, bird sanctuaries... The most remarkable monument in the woods is the Château de Vincennes,who is an marvellous example of medieval architecture, which can be visited every day.
